Exercise Plan
A Workout Plan for You
Day 1: Lower Body Focus

Barbell Hip Thrusts - 4 sets x 10 reps

Sumo Deadlifts - 4 sets x 8 reps

Walking Lunges with Dumbbells - 3 sets x 12 steps each leg

Leg Press - 3 sets x 12 reps

Cable Pull-throughs- 3 sets x 12 reps

Dumbbell Seated Calf Raises - 3 sets x 15 reps

Alternative Day 1 Routine
Day 2: Glute and Core Focus

Glute Kickbacks (using cable machine or resistance bands) - 4 sets x 12 reps each leg

Bulgarian Split Squats - 4 sets x 10 reps each leg

Cable Woodchoppers - 3 sets x 12 reps each side

Plank with Leg Lifts - 3 sets x 12 reps each leg

Russian Twists with Medicine Ball - 3 sets x 15 reps each side

Lat Pulldown - 3 sets x 10 reps

Day 3: Full Body Circuit

Barbell Squats - 4 sets x 8 reps

Romanian Deadlifts - 4 sets x 10 reps

Bench Press - 4 sets x 8 reps

Dumbbell Flyes - 3 sets x 10 reps

Push-ups (can be modified on knees if needed) - 3 sets x 12 reps

Lateral Lunges - 3 sets x 12 reps each leg

Dumbbell Shoulder Press - 3 sets x 10 reps
